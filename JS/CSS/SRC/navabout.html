About US

PickupBiz business philosophy is to assure the highest quality product, total client satisfaction, timely delivery of solutions and the best quality price ratio found in the industry. Our emphasis is on offering a high degree of product user friendliness through a positive, creative and focused company staff. Trust, Integrity and Teamwork are the values upon which we built our growth and success, and which we live and work.

PickupBiz is online/offline bootcamp for digital economy skills training focused on helping people acquire the skills they need to thrive in the Digital World. We provide rigorous online/offline training in disciplines such as Web Development, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science, among others. In other words, we specialize in areas where Technologies and best practices are changing rapidly, and the demand for qualified candidates significantly exceeds supply.
